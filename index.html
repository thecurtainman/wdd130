Virginia

Virginia is a state in the eastern United States. It is bordered by Maryland to the north, Washington D.C. and the Atlantic Ocean to the east, North Carolina and Tennessee to the south, as well as Kentucky and West Virginia to the west. The state is a center of government, transportation, education, information technology, research, and a major tourist destination for outdoor recreation. Richmond is the state capital and largest city.
